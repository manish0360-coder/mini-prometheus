The frozen vocabulary (HANDBOOK_v1.1 §1.6, §1.10, N.1; Appendix A):

- **Three representational domains** (§1.6): **Reality** (the external world and its artifacts),
  **Cognitive State** (transient working representations of an active episode), and the
  **Structured Generative World Model** (persistent learned knowledge).
- **Mechanism vs. Content** (§1.10): *Mechanism* = how something is done, domain-independent
  (Noetica's property). *Content* = what is remembered/planned/verified/reasoned about (a domain's).
- **Interface vs. Implementation** (§1.10): typed versioned contracts published by Noetica vs.
  concrete domain classes that satisfy them.
- **State substrate** (§1.10): the persistent, typed, provenance-tracked representation that is the
  platform core. **Episode, Verdict, Provenance** as defined in §1.10.
- **Canonical taxonomy** (N.1): four layers — MiniFlyWire, Noetica, Velith, Mini Prometheus — plus
  "AI Manufacturing Intelligence" as the outcome, not a layer.

Note (Constitutional Evolution Report Part 5): "Situation State" and "Constraint Network" are **not**
canonical ontology terms; they are reconciled to manufacturing content.

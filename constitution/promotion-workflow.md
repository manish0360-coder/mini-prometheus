# Promotion Workflow

> **Authoritative source: `constitution/HANDBOOK_v1.1.md` (Constitution v1.1).** This file is a cited
> extraction for navigation; on any discrepancy the Handbook governs (P.2, Law 23). Amend only by CAP.

Two transfer mechanisms, never conflated (HANDBOOK_v1.1 §11.3–§11.4, Laws 7–8):

- **Promotion (research → platform)** — a *validated mechanism* moves from MiniFlyWire into Noetica by
  **re-implementation behind a versioned interface**, never by import (Law 7). Gate: certified by the
  Research Director/Scientific Council; entered in the Primitive Registry (§11.5).
- **Extraction (application → platform)** — a *proven mechanism* is refactored up from a domain
  application (first Velith) into Noetica once it has a real consumer and a credible second (Law 8).
  Speculative extraction is rejected.

The reusable loop machinery of the Velith spike (verifier harness, episode store, content-hash, LLM
adapter, held-out lock) is obtained by **extraction** into Noetica (N.3).

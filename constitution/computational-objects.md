Canonical objects are Noetica-layer (HANDBOOK_v1.1 §1.10, Part VI):

- **State Substrate** — persistent, typed, provenance-tracked representation; the platform core
  (§1.10; DECISIONS D9). Noetica mechanism; domains *use* it (Law 6).
- **WorldModel** — a Noetica-published interface (§1.10); engineering content is Velith's, the
  manufacturing twin content is Mini Prometheus's (§2.4/§8.4).
- **Episode** — provenance-complete, content-hashed record of one grounded attempt (§1.10).
- **Verdict** — grounded verification outcome; closed taxonomy (§1.10; Velith DECISIONS D16.7/D17).
- **Provenance** — derivation of every belief; first-class (§1.10, Law 18).
- **Knowledge Store** — typed graph store engine (§6.5); constraints live here as *content*.

Mini Prometheus owns none of these mechanisms; it owns manufacturing **content** instantiated on
them (Constitutional Evolution Report Part 3; CAP-0001 Field 7).
